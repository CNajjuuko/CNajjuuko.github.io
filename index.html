Background

About Me

I am part of the AI for Health Institute Lab at WashU. My work focuses on leveraging advanced computational methods, including machine learning predictive models and large language models, to drive innovation in healthcare delivery and improve public health outcomes. I am particularly interested in global health, child health, HIV care, and healthcare-associated infections surveillance.
